Odins Best Protein Bagels

    Ingredients
  • 1 cup non fat greek yogurt
  • 1 cup self rising flour
  • Note if you dont have self rising flour, add 1 tsp of baking soda and 1/4 tsp of salt

    1. Mix yogurt and flour.
    2. Knead and cut into four equal pieces.
    3. Roll into bagel shape and use and wash to cover the tops and add seasoning of choice.
    4. Bake at 375F for 20-25 minutes.
    5. Let rest for 2-3 minutes and enjoy.
