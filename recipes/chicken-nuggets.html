Odins Best High Protein Chicken Nuggets

    Ingredients
  • 350g of chicken ground or minced (can substitute ground turkey)
  • 3 plain rice cakes
  • salt, pepper, paprika, cajun, garlic
  • 1 egg

    1. Add 1 egg and 1 tsp of salt, pepper, garlic to the chicken and mix together.
    2. Blend the rice cakes with a tsp of all the spices.
    3. Divide the chicken into pieces. Then dip the chicken in the rice cake mix.
    4. Spread evenly in air-fryer.
    5. Cook for 10-12 minutes in air-fryer or until fully cooked.
    6. Let rest for 2-3mins, then enjoy!
