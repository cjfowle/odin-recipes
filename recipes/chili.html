Odins Best Chili Recipe

    Ingredients
  • 1 pound ground beef (can substitue turkey or venison)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (can substitute avocado oil)
  • 1 can red kidney beans
  • 1 can of pinto or great northern beans
  • 1.5 Cups of Beef Broth
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2.5 tablespoon chili powder
  • 2.5 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon salt and pepper
  • 1 ground cumin
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 (15 oz.) can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 (8 oz.) can tomato sauce
  • Ground Beef

    1. Add olive oil to deep pot over medium-high heat for two minutes. Add the onion. Cook for 5 minutes, and make sure to stir occassionally.
    2. Add ground beef or substitute to the pot. Use wooden spoon to break apart the meat. Cook for 7 minutes, or until meat is browned. Make sure to stir occassionally.
    3. Add in chili powder, honey, garlic powder, salt and pepper, cumin, and tomato paste. Stir until evenly combined.
    4. Add the broth, diced tomatoes, drained and rinsed beans, and tomato sauce. Stir until combineds.
    5. Bring liquid to a slow and low boil. Then reduce heat to (low to medium-low) and let simmer for 20-25 minutes. Stir occasionally.
    6. Remove the pot from the heat. Let chili sit for 5-10 minutes.
    7. Serve chili
    8. If you are going to freeze for later, first let chili cool off then use quart sized freezer bags to fill with chili. Then stack evenly in freezer.
    9. When unfreezing, let chili freezer bag soak in a bowl of water until thawed out and put in a microwave safe bowl to heat up.
